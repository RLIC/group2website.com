Scenario

Ito si Kuya Tami ng Sitio Bebe, araw-araw kinakailangan niyang mag-igib sa poso. Kailangan itong pakuluan upang may maiinom na malinis na tubig. Isang maliit na bayan ang kasalukuyang humaharap sa matinding krisis pangkalusugan. Ang bayan na ito ay hindi malapit sa natural na daluyan ng tubig kaya madalas ay nag-aangkat ang lokal na pamahalaan ng malinis na tubig para sa mga residente, o kaya naman ay nagbubukas ng mga balon. Dahil na rin dito, madalas na may tagtuyot sa lugar lalo kung tag-init. Karamihan sa populasyon ay mga manggagawa. Ang karaniwang kabuhayan ng mga residente dito ay pagmimina at pagtatrabaho sa pabrika. Mayaman sa human resources ang lugar sapagkat masisipag ang mga residente rito maging mga bata man o matanda. Madali rin silang turuan ng mga kasanayan na kinakailangan para sa kanilang pangkabuhayan. Gayunpaman, nakakaranas ng kakulangan sa health professionals ang nasabing lugar kung kaya’t madalang din na may malapitan ang mga residente sa tuwing may nagkakasakit. Ayon sa Kagawaran ng Kalusugan (Department of Health), nakapagtala ang Sitio Bebe ng sumusunod na datos ng 2019:. Talahanayan 1. Bilang ng naitalang kaso ng mga sakit sa Sitio Bebe noong 2019.

Concert Plans

Pre Concert Preparation

  1. Venue - The GT-Toyota Asian Center Auditorium has a floor area of 23 m x 15 m excluding the stage, and a capacity of 460 guests. Renting the venue costs Php27,500 for the first three hours, and Php5,300 for every succeeding hour or fraction thereof.
  2. Performers - Ace of Spare Acoustic and Kelvin Masangkay

Concert Proper

Before we start the program we will be telling our guests what they signed up for, informing them about the case of Sitio bebe, its problems, and what this concert will do to help them, we then continue on with the introduction of the performances that is served. Shortly after the introduction the MT’s (Us) will now introduce the first performer that will perform their first performance, the Ace of Spare Acoustic, subsequently the MT’s will now go back to the stage follwing the exit of the Ace of Spare Acoustic, to check up on the crowds, hype them up, and praise the performer, Following this the MT’s will now introduce the second performer, Kelvin Masangkay, for his first performance with the flute, similar with what the MT’s did after the first performer’s first performance, the MT’s will check up on the Crowds make sure that they feel the right Vibes, make some hugot, as long as it is relevant to what Kelvin played. To keep things up and running the MT’s will now changed with the other set MT’s, this is to make sure that the crowd doesn’t get bored and just to switch things up a notch, to give them more options, at the same time the new set of MT’s will introduce back again the first performer, Ace of Spare Acoustic, for their second song for the concert, and then following what the first set of MT’s did when a performance ended, hence the MT’s will now announce the second performers last performance, after the performance has ended the MT’s will hype up the crowds, check up on them for the last time, finally at the end of the concert all MT’s will stand up on the stage, introducing for the last and finally time the Performers who performed in the concert for a duet/collab finall performance, ending the concert, with thanks and gratitude for the people who came.

Post Concert Tasks

When the concert ends we are going to count the total collected funds and distribute it. After we are able to count the total amount of the total collected funds we are going to pay the venue and the performers with the collected funds. The next thing we are going to do is to count the remaining funds and distribute it into two. The first half is going to focus on their health to help them build some wells, the other half is going to focus on the health professionals because they don’t have enough health professionals. Lastly we are going to give them the separated funds to Sitio Bebe
