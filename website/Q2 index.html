Advertising Plan

Our group's Brand name/Youtube Channel Name, is Anzen. We decided this name by just thinking of a word that is simple and easy to remember and not too complicated to understand. So we brainstorm and recall the scenario during quarter 1. Our scenario is to help Sitio Bebe with their health issues because they don’t have enough health professionals. So we decided Anzen because if we translate Safe/Secure in English to Japanese word the word will be Anzen.

Our group plans to keep it simple and at the same time show what we do and to make our target audiences remember us by seeing just our simple logo. We also included our brand name, slogan in our logo. So just in case one of our target audience forgot how our logo looks he/she can read our brand name to identify and remember us.

Our group's tagline/slogan is Great Helps. Great Times. Our group just researched for some examples, and we took inspiration from one of the taglines we’ve seen. Also this tagline gives an idea for the things we will do, for example great help it shows that we will help, and by helping each other we can have great times.
